Custom Attorney List Building

Your criteria, our research

Every Lexica order is a custom build. There's no self-serve platform where you download a pre-made list. You tell us exactly what you need, and we build it from scratch using bar records and 50+ verified sources.

Common custom criteria include: specific practice areas or subcategories, geographic territories (state, metro, ZIP code), firm size, seniority level, years admitted, and multi-state bar admissions.

How it works

Send us your criteria via the contact form or email. We'll scope the project, provide a record count estimate and quote, and deliver your list within 3-5 business days of approval. Complex builds with unusual criteria may take 5-7 days.

Examples

  • "Managing partners at employment law firms with 10-50 attorneys in Illinois"
  • "Patent prosecution attorneys in California, Texas, and New York"
  • "Insurance defense attorneys at firms with verified emails in the Southeast"
  • "All bankruptcy attorneys admitted to practice in Delaware"

If you can describe it, we can build it. The more specific your criteria, the more targeted your outreach.

Frequently Asked Questions

What does the custom list building service include?

Each record includes the attorney's full name, verified email, direct phone number, firm name, firm address, bar number, bar status, practice area classification, and seniority level. Custom fields are available on request.

How is the data verified?

We source raw data from state bar associations, then cross-reference against court records, business filings, and firm websites. Emails are deliverability-tested. Phone numbers are verified against active carrier records. The full process takes 3-5 business days per list.

How long does delivery take?

Most orders are delivered in 3-5 business days. Complex builds with custom criteria may take 5-7 days. Rush delivery is available.
