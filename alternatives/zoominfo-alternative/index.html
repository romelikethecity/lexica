Alternative

ZoomInfo Alternative for Law Firm Data

Why teams look for alternatives

Enterprise B2B platforms charge $15K-$30K/year for access to a database of 100M+ contacts. If you only need attorney data, you're paying for 99% of contacts you'll never use. Opt-in directories only cover attorneys who chose to list themselves, missing the majority of the market.

What Lexica offers instead

Lexica is a managed data service, not a platform. You describe your target legal market. We build a bar-verified list from state bar records and deliver it as a CSV. Every attorney is license-verified. Every email is deliverability-tested. Every firm is classified by practice area and size.

Pricing is $0.50-$1.50 per record, per project. No annual contract. A targeted list of 1,000 attorneys costs $500-$1,500, not $15,000.

What Lexica doesn't do

We're not a platform. We don't offer intent data, workflow automation, or email sequencing. If you need those features, keep your existing tools. Use Lexica for the attorney contact data, and run your campaigns through whatever platform you already use.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Lexica a full replacement?

Lexica replaces the attorney-specific data. It doesn't replace platform features like workflow automation. Many teams use Lexica for contacts alongside lighter-weight sales tools.

Can I try before committing?

Yes. We can build a free sample matched to your criteria so you can evaluate the data quality before purchasing.

How fast can I get data?

Most orders are delivered in 3-5 business days. Free samples take 2-3 days.
