Attorney Data for Legal Staffing

Place temp attorneys and review teams. Built for companies like Contract attorney and document review staffing agencies.

The problem

Legal staffing requires knowing which attorneys are available, what their bar admissions cover, and which firms need temporary help. Generic databases don't track bar status, multi-state admissions, or contract availability.

How Lexica solves this

Lexica provides bar-verified attorney contacts with state admission data and practice area classification. Identify firms with the size and practice mix that typically use contract attorneys. Source candidates with specific bar admissions for the jurisdictions your clients need.

What you get

A clean CSV with every field you need to run targeted campaigns. Attorney name, verified email address, direct phone number, firm name and full address, state bar number with active status, and practice area classification. No platform to log into. No annual contract to sign.

You tell us your target criteria: practice areas, geographic territory, firm size, seniority level. We build the list from state bar records and 50+ verified sources. Every record is deliverability-tested before it reaches your inbox.

Frequently Asked Questions

What data does Lexica provide for legal staffing firms?

You get the attorney's name, a verified email address, a direct phone number, the firm name and full address, their state bar number with active status, and their practice area category. Additional data points like years admitted or multi-state bar admissions are available as add-ons.

How is the data verified?

Verification runs in three stages: bar status check against state records, contact validation (email deliverability + phone carrier check), and firm confirmation via business listing cross-reference. Only records that pass all three stages make it into your final list.

How fresh is the data?

We build every list on demand. There's no stale database sitting on a server. Bar data is pulled from state associations at the time of your order, and contact details are verified before delivery. Most data is less than two weeks old when you receive it.

What practice areas can I filter by?

Fourteen top-level practice area categories with subtypes for the largest areas. Corporate alone breaks into M&A, venture capital, securities, governance, and private equity. Litigation breaks into commercial, class action, appellate, product liability, and insurance coverage.

How long does delivery take?

Most orders are delivered in 3-5 business days. Complex builds with custom criteria may take 5-7 days. Rush delivery is available for time-sensitive campaigns.
