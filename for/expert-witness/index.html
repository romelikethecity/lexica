Attorney Data for Expert Witness Firms

Reach trial lawyers who hire experts. Built for companies like Expert Institute, SEAK, Round Table Group, IMS Consulting.

The problem

Expert witness placement depends on reaching the right litigation attorneys at the right time. Generic contact lists include transactional lawyers, government attorneys, and retired practitioners who will never hire an expert.

How Lexica solves this

Lexica provides bar-verified contacts for active litigation attorneys segmented by practice area and seniority. Target the partners who make retention decisions, not associates who forward emails. Filter by firm size to match your pricing model.

What you get

A clean CSV with every field you need to run targeted campaigns. Attorney name, verified email address, direct phone number, firm name and full address, state bar number with active status, and practice area classification. No platform to log into. No annual contract to sign.

You tell us your target criteria: practice areas, geographic territory, firm size, seniority level. We build the list from state bar records and 50+ verified sources. Every record is deliverability-tested before it reaches your inbox.

Frequently Asked Questions

What data does Lexica provide for expert witness companies?

Each record includes the attorney's full name, verified email, direct phone number, firm name, firm address, bar number, bar status, practice area classification, and seniority level. Custom fields are available on request.

How is the data verified?

We source raw data from state bar associations, then cross-reference against court records, business filings, and firm websites. Emails are deliverability-tested. Phone numbers are verified against active carrier records. The full process takes 3-5 business days per list.

How fresh is the data?

Lists are built fresh for each order. We don't sell pre-built databases. When you place an order, we pull the latest bar records and run verification at that point. Typical data age at delivery is under 30 days for bar status and under 7 days for contact verification.

What practice areas can I filter by?

We segment by 14 major practice areas (corporate, litigation, IP, real estate, employment, PI, criminal defense, family, bankruptcy, immigration, tax, environmental, healthcare, insurance defense) and 15+ subtypes within the larger categories.

How long does delivery take?

Most orders are delivered in 3-5 business days. Complex builds with custom criteria may take 5-7 days. Rush delivery is available for time-sensitive campaigns.
