Large Law Firm Contact Data

Large regional and national firms with multiple offices and practice groups. Complex org structures where reaching the right decision-maker matters.

What's included

Bar-verified attorney contacts at firms matching this size segment. Each record includes attorney name, verified email, direct phone, firm name and address, bar number with active status, practice area classification, and seniority level.

Firm size classification is determined by cross-referencing bar association records, firm websites, and business registration data. We verify attorney counts at the time of list build, not from stale directory data.

Why firm size matters

Your product's pricing model, implementation requirements, and sales cycle all vary by firm size. A solo practitioner evaluates software differently than a 100-attorney firm. Lexica lets you target the exact firm segment where your product fits, so your outreach lands with the right audience.

Frequently Asked Questions

What data do you have for large firms (51-200)?

You get the attorney's name, a verified email address, a direct phone number, the firm name and full address, their state bar number with active status, and their practice area category. Additional data points like years admitted or multi-state bar admissions are available as add-ons.

How do you verify attorney data?

Verification runs in three stages: bar status check against state records, contact validation (email deliverability + phone carrier check), and firm confirmation via business listing cross-reference. Only records that pass all three stages make it into your final list.

Can I combine firm size with practice area filters?

Yes. You can request a list filtered by firm size and practice area simultaneously. For example, managing partners at employment law firms with 10-50 attorneys in California. Custom combinations are standard.
