Frequently Asked Questions

What practice areas do you cover?

We segment by 14 major practice areas (corporate, litigation, IP, real estate, employment, PI, criminal defense, family, bankruptcy, immigration, tax, environmental, healthcare, insurance defense) and 15+ subtypes within the larger categories.

How do you verify bar status?

We source raw data from state bar associations, then cross-reference against court records, business filings, and firm websites. Emails are deliverability-tested. Phone numbers are verified against active carrier records. The full process takes 3-5 business days per list.

Can I filter by firm size or attorney seniority?

Yes. You can filter by firm size (solo, small, mid-size, large, Am Law 200), practice area, geography, seniority level, and custom criteria. Every order is built to your specifications.

What data fields are included?

Each record includes the attorney's full name, verified email, direct phone number, firm name, firm address, bar number, bar status, practice area classification, and seniority level. Custom fields are available on request.

How is this different from Martindale-Hubbell?

Martindale-Hubbell is an opt-in directory. Attorneys choose whether to list themselves, and the data is self-reported. Lexica covers every licensed attorney in America by sourcing from state bar records directly. We don't rely on attorneys opting in, so our coverage is comprehensive, not partial.

How long does delivery take?

Most orders are delivered in 3-5 business days. Complex builds with custom criteria or very large geographies may take 5-7 days. Rush delivery is available for time-sensitive projects.

Is there a minimum order?

No minimum. You can order a list for a single metro area or an entire state. Pricing is per-project, based on the size and complexity of your request.
